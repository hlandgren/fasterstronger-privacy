Faster Stronger

Privacy Policy — Effective March 15, 2026

Overview

Faster Stronger is a personal health coaching app that analyzes your Apple Health data to provide insights, trends, and advice. Your privacy is fundamental to how we built this app.

Data We Access

With your explicit permission, Faster Stronger reads the following data from Apple HealthKit:

  • Step count and walking/running distance
  • Active energy burned and exercise minutes
  • Heart rate and resting heart rate
  • Sleep analysis
  • Flights climbed
  • Weight and body measurements

We also access your device microphone if you choose to use the voice coaching feature.

How Your Data Is Used

  • On-device analysis: Your health data is processed on your device to compute trends, segments, and statistics.
  • AI-powered insights: Aggregated health summaries (not raw data points) are sent to AI services (OpenAI, Anthropic, or Berget) to generate personalized coaching messages and health narratives.
  • No raw health data is stored on external servers. AI providers receive only summarized statistics needed to generate insights.

Data Storage

  • All health data remains in Apple HealthKit on your device.
  • Cached insights and coaching messages are stored locally on your device.
  • We do not maintain any external database of your health information.
  • We do not have user accounts or authentication systems.

Third-Party Services

Faster Stronger uses the following third-party AI services to generate health insights:

  • OpenAI — for health analysis and coaching (openai.com/privacy)
  • Anthropic — for health analysis (anthropic.com/privacy)
  • ElevenLabs — for voice coaching (elevenlabs.io/privacy)

These services receive only aggregated health summaries, not raw HealthKit data. We recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal health data with any third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. We do not use any analytics or tracking SDKs.

Your Control

  • You can revoke HealthKit access at any time in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Health.
  • You can delete locally cached data by deleting the app.
  • All HealthKit permissions are granular — you choose which data types to share.

Children's Privacy

Faster Stronger is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
